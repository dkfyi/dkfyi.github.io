Hydra Management Structure

Hydra DC is managed by a two-level structure: the Executive Committee (EC), which will include the management of the company, and the Decentralized Board of Token Owners (TOB), which will include all owners of Hydra (HDT) tokens. The interaction between the EC and TOB will be built on bilateral interaction, similar to the interaction between the executive board and the supervisory board in traditional corporations.

To make significant decisions for the company, the Hydra Executive Committee will put on vote by TOB calculated and well-designed decisions that guarantee the company's profit, and the TOB will approve or disapprove of the specific proposals of the EC. Such a system of double control will ensure stability in the development of the company and protection from corporate trolls.

It is TOB that will decide how much of Hydra's profit will be directed to the development of the project, and which - to pay dividends. And the first decision after the Crowdsale, which will be taken by TOB, will be the choice of the location of the DC location.

About the Executive committee

The Hydra Executive Committee will prepare its own management decisions, analyze TOB’s proposals and put to the vote the TOB acceptable to the company development strategies and initiatives, the risks of which are analyzed with the help of IBM Watson A.i.

The membership of EC members will be formed on the basis of professional skills relevant for business and technology (IT development, IT security, network engineering, business process management, etc.).

About the Token Owners Board

The decentralized Board of Token Owners (TOB) is the central authority of Hydra, which will include every holder of HDT tokens. TOB will coordinate the decisions of the EC, which are optimal for the development of the company.

Holders of HDT tokens within the framework of TOB will have the right to vote on managerial decisions within the company, will have the opportunity to offer their own development strategies for the company. The strength of the voice inside the TOB will be determined by the number of HDT tokens.

HDT tokens will not only influence the management of the company, but will be freely traded on exchanges, also they will be the basis for receiving dividends from the company's profits, used to purchase Hydra DC services.

The Executive Committee of Hydra will be represented in TOB with 20% of voting tokens, which could be used only in voting process and couldn’t be sold on exchanges Thus, Executive Committee will participate in TOB on a par with other token owners.

Data center ownership

From the moment the first profits are received, the owners of HDT tokens will receive dividends. First as members of TOB, and after as shareholders of the company.

At the development stage, when stable operation of the company will be established, the HDT tokens will be converted into shares of the managing company Hydra, registered within territory with British law (will simplify the system of distribution of shares). Owners of HDT tokens will become full shareholders of the data center, and any court operating under the island law will be able to protect their rights.

Owners of HDT tokens en masse will receive a 49% stake in the management company, in proportion to the possession of the tokens. The Hydra Executive Committee will receive 51% of the shares.
